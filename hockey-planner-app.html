Hockey Planner App.

Hockey Planner is an app where hockey players can do availability check for a training or a match. They collect points for attending these sports moments and can win prizes at the end of a season. My role as A UX/UI designer in this project was to do user research, create a brand and translate that research into a working prototype.

The Problem.

At hockeyclub the Reigers lower teams have a way lower attendency compared to the first teams. This is especially the case at a training. Only 58% of the players attend a training on average per week.

Key Insights.
