About me.

Hi again, my name is Thomas

I'm a junior UX/UI designer living in Hoofddorp the Netherlands, interested in how to make better experiences for people online.

In 2019 I graduated as a Interactive Designer at the Media College based in Amsterdam. Later that year I got my first job as a Junior UX/UI designer at a small tech start-up called Hybriq.

Now I'm looking for a new adventure as a Junior UX/UI designer. I want to push my boundries and learn more about design thinking, UI design, user research and even some coding.
