About me

I am a 25-year-old junior UI / UX designer currently looking for a full-time job around Amsterdam. I started my study Design For Interaction at the Media College Amsterdam in 2015 and I graduated in 2019. 

Right after my graduation, I worked for 1 year as a UI / UX designer at a small start-up called Hybriq. After that year I took a break because of the COVID pandemic, and I wanted to complete my portfolio first.

I am an extrovert person with a strong preference to be amongst people and to meet colleagues in person as this leads to the best possible design results. 

I am eager and motivated to take on a new challenge where I can add value and learn a lot of new things!
